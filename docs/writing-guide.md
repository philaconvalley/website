# Writing Guide

Want to share your voice on PhilaCon Valley? This guide gives you everything you need to get started -- topic ideas, templates, and how to submit.

You do not need to be a professional writer. You do not need to be an expert. If you have something to share, we want to hear it.

## Two Ways to Contribute

### 1. Link to your own platform (easiest)

Already have a Substack, Medium, YouTube, podcast, or blog? We will spotlight it on our Community Voices page. Just [open a submission](https://github.com/philaconvalley/website/issues/new?template=blog_submission.md) with the link.

### 2. Write something for the site

Want to publish directly on philaconvalley.com? Write it up and we will help you get it live. Use the templates below as a starting point.

## Topic Ideas

Not sure what to write about? Here are some ideas grouped by type. Pick one that resonates -- or come up with your own.

### Your Story

- How I got into tech (career changers welcome)
- What I wish I knew when I started coding
- My experience at a PhilaCon Valley event
- What it is like being [your identity] in tech in Philly
- A mistake I made and what I learned from it

### Technical

- A beginner-friendly explainer of something you just learned
- How I built [project] -- a walkthrough
- Tools and setup that made my life easier
- A concept I struggled with and how I finally understood it
- Comparing two technologies and when to use each

### Career

- How I prepared for technical interviews
- Resume tips that actually worked for me
- What I look for when reviewing portfolios
- Navigating the job search as a self-taught developer
- How I negotiated my salary (or wish I had)

### Community

- Recap of a PhilaCon Valley event
- Spotlight on a community member
- A project we built together at Collab Lab
- Why open source matters and how to start
- Resources that helped me level up

### Philadelphia

- Why Philly is a great place to build a tech career
- Tech companies and startups in Philadelphia worth knowing
- Free or affordable tech resources in the city
- Community spaces and meetups beyond PhilaCon Valley

## Templates

### Personal Story Template

```
Title: [Your title here]
Description: [One sentence summary]

## The Setup
What was your situation? What were you doing before?

## The Turning Point
What changed? What happened?

## What I Learned
What did you take away from this experience?

## Advice for Others
What would you tell someone in a similar situation?
```

### Technical Walkthrough Template

```
Title: [Your title here]
Description: [One sentence summary]

## What We Are Building / Learning
Brief overview of the topic.

## Prerequisites
What does the reader need to know or have installed?

## Step by Step
Walk through the process. Use code snippets where helpful.

## Common Pitfalls
What might trip someone up?

## Next Steps
Where can the reader go from here?
```

### Event Recap Template

```
Title: [Event Name] Recap - [Date]
Description: [One sentence summary of what happened]

## What Happened
Overview of the event -- what was the theme, who was there?

## Highlights
What were the best moments? What did people build or learn?

## Key Takeaways
What should someone who missed it know?

## Next Event
When and where is the next one? How to RSVP.
```

### Community Spotlight Template

```
Title: Meet [Name] - [Their Role or Focus]
Description: [One sentence about who they are]

## Who They Are
Brief intro -- who is this person and what do they do?

## Their Journey
How did they get into tech? What is their path?

## What They Are Working On
Current projects, interests, or goals.

## Their Advice
What would they tell someone starting out?

## Where to Find Them
Links to their social media, portfolio, or projects.
```

## Writing Tips

- **Write like you talk.** This is a community blog, not an academic paper.
- **Start with a story.** People connect with experiences, not abstractions.
- **Keep it focused.** One idea per post. If it is getting long, split it into a series.
- **Use short paragraphs.** Walls of text are hard to read on screens.
- **Include specifics.** "I spent 3 months learning React" is better than "I learned a framework."
- **It does not need to be perfect.** We will help you edit. Just get the ideas down.

## How to Submit

1. **Easiest**: [Open a blog submission issue](https://github.com/philaconvalley/website/issues/new?template=blog_submission.md) with your link or draft
2. **If you write code**: Create a `.md` file in `src/content/blog/` and open a PR (see [Adding Content](adding-content.md))
3. **Not sure?** Email waskar@philaconvalley.com or reach out on Slack

## Questions?

- Not sure if your topic fits? Ask -- it probably does.
- Need help editing? We are happy to help.
- Want to co-write with someone? We can connect you.

Every voice matters. We want to hear yours.
